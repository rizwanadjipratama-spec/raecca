### 📝 THE PROMPT (Copy & Paste this into Stitch):

> **Role & Goal:** You are a world-class UI/UX Designer who frequently wins Site-of-the-Day on Awwwards. Design a stunning, interactive desktop landing page for a cosmetic brand called "Raecca", focusing on their glossy lip product "Over The Glaze".
> 
> **Art Direction & Aesthetic:** 
> - **Theme:** Y2K Coquette, Hyper-feminine, Editorial Japanese Magazine, Glossy & Juicy.
> - **Color Palette:** Soft cream/beige for the main page background (to give a warm, premium feel). Use pastel baby pinks, vibrant hot magenta, and soft reds for the UI elements and cards.
> - **Typography:** Mix bold, chunky 3D/bubbly fonts (for impact) with elegant, thin serif or cursive fonts (for luxury/editorial feel). 
> 
> **Specific UI Elements to Generate:**
> 1. **Hero Section:** A visually striking introduction. Floating 3D glossy lip-tint bottles (with heart-shaped caps). Include a bold headline: "Find Your PERFECT LIPPIES" using a mix of bubbly and cursive typography.
> 2. **Editorial Product Carousel (Crucial):** Emulate the provided reference layout. Create a horizontal section with 3 to 4 distinct "Product Cards". 
>    - The cards should have a soft pastel pink background with a delicate border.
>    - Place a perfectly cut-out, high-quality 3D glossy cosmetic product right in the center of each card.
>    - Add "Magazine" details: Put small text like "[2026 Issue]" in the top right corner.
>    - Use overlapping typography for the product names (e.g., "Over Cute" or "Bunny Pink") where a bubbly font sits behind an elegant cursive font.
> 3. **Texture & Details:** Incorporate subtle heart motifs, soft drop shadows, and glassmorphism (frosted glass) effects on navigation buttons or floating badges (e.g., "New Arrival 🌸").
> 
> **Output:** A high-fidelity UI mockup of the website landing page. Ensure the layout feels spacious, breathable, asymmetric, and ready for high-end smooth scrolling web animations. Do NOT make it look like a standard Shopify template. Make it look like a digital art piece.
